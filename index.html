About Us

Vision International Company is a staffing agency based in Gisozi, Kigali. We focus on connecting skilled workers with employers in need of household, personal, and business support services.

Our mission is to reduce unemployment by providing accessible and fair opportunities while making it easier for families and businesses to find the help they need.
